Program Overview

The Men's Group Coaching ("the Program") is a group coaching experience designed to support men in areas including mindset, purpose, relationships, accountability, and personal growth. The Program is facilitated by Men's Group Coaching ("the Coach" or "we"). Contact: mensgroupcoach@gmail.com  ·  561-677-0094.

Program details including format, schedule, and content are as follows:

  • Format: Live group sessions (virtual or in-person, as specified at enrollment)
  • Duration: As outlined in your enrollment materials or sales agreement
  • Session frequency: As communicated upon enrollment
  • Platform: FaceTime and Zoom

Participant Responsibilities
