04 — The Process

First 90 Days,
step by step.

Real change doesn't happen in a weekend. Here's the actual cadence — what we build, when we build it, and how the work compounds.

Week 1–2

Foundation

Define the niche of your goals. Audit current state — sleep, training, nutrition, recovery. Set up systems for tracking. Establish baseline metrics so we know exactly what's moving.

Week 3–4

Launch & Calibrate

Custom training program goes live. Nutrition framework dialed in. We run the first full cycle and adjust based on real feedback — your body, your schedule, your life.

Month 2

Compound

Habits become identity. Energy returns. Sleep restructures. We refine the program based on what's working, kill what isn't, and build momentum into the next phase.

Month 3

Embed

The protocol stops being something you "do" and starts being who you are. We finalize your independent training framework so the work continues long after coaching ends.

Start before you're ready. You don't need a perfect plan, a gym membership, or the right shoes. You need a clear offer, a genuine desire to change, and the courage to begin.
